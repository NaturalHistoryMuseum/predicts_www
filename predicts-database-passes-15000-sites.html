PREDICTS database passes 15,000 sites

Date Wed 07 May 2014

In the last few minutes, the database has passed 15,000 sites. Helen Phillips uploaded data from Gray et al.'s recent paper on dung beetle assemblages in Sabah. This is also the 21st dataset we have from Malaysia, moving it level with Canada and only one behind the UK and the USA - but still some way behind Brazil, which leads the table with 40 studies.
