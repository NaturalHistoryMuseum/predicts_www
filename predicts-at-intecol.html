PREDICTS at INTECOL

Date Thu 08 August 2013

PREDICTS will be well represented at INTECOL, with five of the team giving talks. Details and links to the abstracts below.

Monday 19th August, 12:30-12:45 (Biogeography & Ecology)

Dom Bennett - Phylogenetic non-randomness in the responses of biodiversity to human impacts: a global analysis of local responses.

Monday 19 August, 16:00-16:15 (Conservation Ecology)

Dan Ingram - Effect of agriculture on local biodiversity in the tropics: A meta-analysis.

Tuesday 20th August, 16:45-17:00 (Speed Science)

Adriana De Palma - Functional and taxonomic diversity of European bees in human-dominated landscapes.

Thursday 22nd August, 11:45-12:00 (Maths, Models and Methods)

Susy Echeverría-Londoño - Modelling and projecting the responses of Colombian biodiversity to human impacts.

Friday 23rd August, 10:15-10:30 (Large-Scale Ecology)

Past, present and future projections of local biodiversity change in response to human drivers, at a global scale.
